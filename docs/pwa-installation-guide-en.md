# Installing Effitrans on Your Device (English guide)

Effitrans installs like an app, with no app store. Installation adds an icon to your home
screen and opens the platform full-screen.

**Important:** installation changes nothing about security — your data stays online and no
operational data is stored offline. Without a network the app shows an "Offline" screen and
**no changes are saved**.

## Android (Chrome)

1. Open `https://effitrans-operations.vercel.app` in Chrome and sign in.
2. An "Installer Effitrans sur cet appareil ?" prompt appears — tap **Installer**.
   (Otherwise: ⋮ menu → **Install app**.)
3. The Effitrans icon appears on your home screen.

## iPhone / iPad (Safari)

Safari shows no automatic prompt:

1. Open the platform in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow).
3. Choose **"Add to Home Screen"**, then **Add**.

## Desktop (Chrome / Edge)

1. Open the platform and sign in.
2. Click the install icon in the address bar (or menu → Install Effitrans).

## Updates

When a new version is deployed, a "**Nouvelle version disponible**" banner appears at the
bottom of the screen. Work in progress is **not** lost until you tap **Mettre à jour**. Tap it
when ready — the app reloads exactly once.

## Offline — what works and what doesn't

- ✅ The installed app opens and shows a clear "Offline" screen.
- ❌ Dossiers, shipments, documents and live data are **not** viewable offline.
- ❌ Nothing entered offline is saved — reconnect to continue.

## Supported browsers

Chrome and Edge (Android + desktop): full installation. Safari iOS/iPadOS: manual installation
above. Firefox: the app works in-browser (installation depends on version).

## Uninstalling

Android: long-press the icon → Uninstall. iOS: long-press → Remove App. Desktop: app window
menu → Uninstall Effitrans.
